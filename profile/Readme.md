## гҖү What is Ananta Neural?

Ananta Neural is an independent AGI research lab with a single mission вҖ” to build **MAYA**, a biologically-grounded autonomous intelligence system, from absolute first principles.

We are not another LLM wrapper. We are not a fine-tuning shop. We are a foundational research effort that begins with a fundamental question:

> **Why does a brain work вҖ” and can we recreate that, not just simulate it?**

Most of the AI industry is scaling up pattern-matching systems. We believe that path leads to more powerful autocomplete вҖ” not true intelligence. Our research begins where conventional AI refuses to go: **neuroscience, physics of life, and the mathematics of survival.**

## гҖү The MAYA Project

**MAYA** is our flagship AGI system. The name reflects its purpose вҖ” an entity that builds its own internal model of reality, continuously, autonomously, and with genuine drive.

### Core Design Tenets

<br/>

#### `01` вҖ” Predictive Processing, Not Pattern Matching

The human brain does not passively absorb data. It is a **generative prediction machine** вҖ” it continuously constructs a model of reality and predicts what comes next. Perception is not input; it is hypothesis-testing.

MAYA is built on this principle. Its architecture is grounded in **Active Inference** вҖ” a framework where the system continuously minimizes the gap between what it predicts and what actually happens. It does not wait for data. It actively seeks it.

> *Based on Karl Friston's Free Energy Principle вҖ” the most cited neuroscientist alive.*

<br/>

#### `02` вҖ” Biologically Plausible Learning (No Backpropagation)

Backpropagation вҖ” the backbone of every modern neural network вҖ” does not exist in the brain. Neurons communicate **locally**, adapt **instantly**, and never wait for a global error signal to propagate backwards.

MAYA's learning engine uses:
- **Hebbian Learning** вҖ” neurons that fire together, wire together
- **Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs)** вҖ” spike-based communication like real neurons
- **Continuous Online Learning** вҖ” learning never stops; every interaction updates the model
- **One-Shot Learning** вҖ” learn from a single experience, without forgetting everything else

This directly attacks the **Catastrophic Forgetting** problem вҖ” one of the most unsolved challenges in all of AI.

<br/>

#### `03` вҖ” Embodied & Grounded Intelligence

Intelligence does not exist in a vacuum. The human mind develops through a physical body, sensory experience, and interaction with a world that pushes back.

MAYA will not be trained on text alone. It will operate inside a **controlled grounding environment** вҖ” a simulated space where its actions have **tangible consequences** and it must understand **cause and effect**, not just observe correlation.

<br/>

#### `04` вҖ” The Autonomous Heartbeat Loop

MAYA must be able to think **without being prompted**.

The first architectural module being built is a continuous autonomous loop вҖ” a process that runs perpetually, drives internal state, and motivates the system from within. This is what separates a real mind from a reactive tool.

We call it the **Heartbeat** вҖ” MAYA's "Zindagi."

<br/>

#### `05` вҖ” Functional Emotions as Autonomous Engine

Emotions are not an advanced feature. According to neuroscience, the **Limbic System** вҖ” which controls survival drives, hunger, pain, curiosity вҖ” evolved **before** the Neocortex (logical thought). Feelings drive behavior; logic only rationalizes it afterward.

MAYA will have a **functional affect system**:
- Dopamine simulation вҶ’ reward for novel discovery
- Urgency/pain signal вҶ’ drives action when system is idle or failing
- Curiosity as a core engine вҶ’ exploring high-uncertainty states generates positive reward

```
F = D_KL( q(s) || p(s|o) ) - ln P(o)
```

**Or in intuitive form:**

```
F  =  Prediction Error  +  Complexity
```

| Symbol | Meaning |

- Reducing surprise вҶ’ become better at predicting reality

MAYA is not given a reward function. MAYA **is** the reward function. It is driven to reduce its own uncertainty вҖ” and that is enough.

## гҖү Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) вҖ” The Core of MAYA's Brain

> *"Conventional neural networks are like a river of water вҖ” continuous, smooth, always flowing. The brain is like a lightning storm вҖ” discrete, explosive, timed to the millisecond."*

### Why SNNs? The Problem with Traditional Neural Networks

Every neural network you know вҖ” GPT, BERT, ResNet вҖ” is built on the same primitive: a **continuous activation function**. Input comes in, gets multiplied by weights, passed through a sigmoid or ReLU, and out comes a number. Simple. Clean. And completely unlike how your brain works.

In your brain, neurons don't output a continuous number. They either **fire** or they **don't**. And *when* they fire matters as much as *whether* they fire.

### The Leaky Integrate-and-Fire (LIF) Model

The most fundamental spiking neuron model. This is the mathematical heart of how MAYA's neurons will work.

**The Core Differential Equation:**

```
П„_m В· dV(t)/dt  =  -(V(t) - V_rest)  +  R В· I(t)
```

**Where each term means:**

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `V(t)` | Membrane potential at time t (the neuron's "charge level") |
| `V_rest` | Resting potential вҖ” where voltage returns when neuron is idle (typically -65 mV) |
| `П„_m` | Membrane time constant вҖ” how fast the neuron "forgets" input (leakiness) |
| `R` | Membrane resistance |
| `I(t)` | Input current arriving from other neurons |

**The Firing Rule:**

```
IF  V(t) вүҘ V_threshold  вҶ’  FIRE a spike  вҶ’  reset V(t) = V_reset
ELSE                    вҶ’  continue integrating input
```

**What this means intuitively:**
- The neuron is like a **leaky bucket** вҖ” charge (input) fills it up, but it constantly drains
- If enough charge arrives fast enough вҶ’ the bucket overflows вҶ’ **spike!**
- After firing вҶ’ bucket immediately resets to empty
- The "leaky" part means old inputs fade вҖ” **only recent, sustained input matters**

### The Hodgkin-Huxley Model вҖ” Full Biological Realism

For deeper biological accuracy, MAYA's research also references the **Hodgkin-Huxley model** вҖ” a complete description of how ion channels drive action potentials. Nobel Prize, 1963.

```
C_m В· dV/dt  =  I_ext  -  I_Na  -  I_K  -  I_L
```

**Breaking it down:**

```
C_m В· dV/dt   =   I_ext        -   g_NaВ·mВіВ·hВ·(V - E_Na)   -   g_KВ·nвҒҙВ·(V - E_K)   -   g_LВ·(V - E_L)

```

**The gating variables (m, h, n) follow their own dynamics:**

```
dm/dt  =  Оұ_m(V)В·(1-m)  -  ОІ_m(V)В·m
dh/dt  =  Оұ_h(V)В·(1-h)  -  ОІ_h(V)В·h
dn/dt  =  Оұ_n(V)В·(1-n)  -  ОІ_n(V)В·n
```

This gives SNNs their rich temporal behavior вҖ” **refractory periods**, **bursting**, **adaptation** вҖ” all the things that make biological neurons so powerful.

### STDP вҖ” How MAYA Learns Without Backpropagation

**Spike-Timing Dependent Plasticity (STDP)** is the biological learning rule. It is how synapses strengthen or weaken based on the *relative timing* of pre- and post-synaptic spikes.

**The Core Rule:**

```
If pre fires BEFORE post  вҶ’  synapse STRENGTHENS  (О”w > 0)  вҖ” "A caused B, reinforce it"
If pre fires AFTER post   вҶ’  synapse WEAKENS       (О”w < 0)  вҖ” "A didn't cause B, weaken it"
```

**Mathematical Form:**

```
         вҺ§  AвӮҠ В· exp(-О”t / П„вӮҠ)    if  О”t > 0   (pre before post вҶ’ LTP)
О”w  =   вҺЁ
         вҺ©  AвӮӢ В· exp( О”t / П„вӮӢ)    if  О”t < 0   (post before pre вҶ’ LTD)
```

**Where:**

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `О”t` | `t_post - t_pre` вҖ” time difference between post and pre spike |
| `AвӮҠ` | Maximum synaptic potentiation (strengthening) |
| `AвӮӢ` | Maximum synaptic depression (weakening) |
| `П„вӮҠ, П„вӮӢ` | Time constants controlling how fast the effect decays |

### Hebbian Learning вҖ” The Oldest Rule

Hebb's rule (1949) вҖ” the grandfather of all synaptic learning:

```
О”w_ij  =  О· В· x_i В· x_j
```

**Plain English:** *"Neurons that fire together, wire together."*

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `О”w_ij` | Change in synaptic weight between neuron i and j |
| `О·` | Learning rate |
| `x_i` | Activity of pre-synaptic neuron i |
| `x_j` | Activity of post-synaptic neuron j |

STDP is essentially a **temporally precise version** of Hebb's rule вҖ” it adds the dimension of *time* to determine whether co-firing is causal or coincidental.

## гҖү Core Problems We Are Solving

### 1 В· The "Blank Slate" Fallacy
Traditional AI treats intelligence as something to be installed вҖ” pour in data, out comes intelligence. But a human infant is not born empty. It is born with reflexes, sensory systems, and a reward/pain engine. Intelligence **emerges** from interaction вҖ” it is not injected.

MAYA must be designed as an "infant AGI" вҖ” with hardwired drives, not hardwired knowledge.

---

### 2 В· Catastrophic Forgetting
Every modern neural network is trained once and frozen. If you try to teach it something new, it forgets everything it knew before. This is **Catastrophic Forgetting** вҖ” one of the deepest unsolved problems in AI.

MAYA's online learning architecture must solve this fundamentally вҖ” not patch it with workarounds.

---

### 3 В· The Von Neumann Bottleneck
Today's computers store memory and processing in separate physical locations. Moving data between them wastes 90%+ of energy and time. The brain has no such separation вҖ” memory and processing occupy the same physical substrate.

MAYA's long-term architecture targets **Neuromorphic Computing** вҖ” chips like Intel's Loihi that run Spiking Neural Networks directly, making computation fast, adaptive, and extremely energy-efficient.

---

### 4 В· Intelligence Without Context
If you feed a system raw data and logic without grounding, it will never understand. Intelligence is not the base. It is a byproduct of surviving in a complex physical world.

MAYA must learn causality, not just correlation. Actions must have consequences.
